Jacobcallisono OS Is Missing or damaged.

recovery options:

Diskette Installation: Insert Disk 32 (OSRECOVERY) In drive A: To recover

CD/DVD Installation: Insert Disc 4 (OS Recovery Process) In drive D: To recover

CF Card Installation: Insert Card 3 (OS Recovery Process 1/2) To Start Recovery

SD Card Installation: Insert Card 2 (OS System Recovery) To recover

MicroSD Card Installation: Insert The OS System Recovery Card To start recovery

USB Stick Installation: Insert USB 2 (Jacobcallisono OS System Recovery) To recover

Hard Disk Installation: Insert a Recovery USB Stick or SD Card To recover

Other options:

To restart, press ALT+B

To change version, press ALT+V

To begin Recovery, press ALT+R
